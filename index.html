Connecting passengers with drivers headed in the same direction, fast, fair, and efficient.

How It Works

For Passengers

  • Enter your pickup and destination points
  • Get matched with drivers going the same way
  • See estimated pickup time and drop-off proximity
  • Pay securely and enjoy the ride

For Drivers

  • Enter your route and availability
  • Get matched with nearby passengers
  • Earn money based on trip distance
  • Only 20% service fee – you keep 80%

Simple Pricing

  • Up to 2km: 2 CHF
  • Up to 5km: 4 CHF
  • Up to 10km: 8 CHF
  • Over 10km: 15 CHF (flat rate)

Drivers keep 80% of the fare – we take a 20% service fee.
